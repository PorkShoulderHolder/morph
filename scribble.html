What's going on here?

This is an attempt at emulating organic scribbles and strokes. The primary goal was to create a rendering process such that the output could not easily be identified as the work of a computer. Makes use of morph.js and swarm.js, two javascript tools I built.

These parameters control the rendering style:


randomness


gravity


friction


sensitivity
